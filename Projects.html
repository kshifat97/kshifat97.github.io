Heat Exchanger

Double Pipe and Shell & Tube Heat Exchanger

In industrial sector, cooling is one of the most important field for smooth operation. So, we undertook a simple and effective cooling system for hot water and other fluids. We made a double pipe heat exchanger by calculating the heat load and the mass flow rate of the hot water. The body was made with cast iron for higher heat transfer and cost minimization.
