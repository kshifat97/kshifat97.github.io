I'am Tahsin

My name is Kazi Tahsin Mahmood. I am currently a student of Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. My major is Mechanical Engineering. I was born in Chittagong of Bangladesh. My primary school is St. Xavier's School and my high school is Bangladesh Nau Bahini School and College. I went to Chittagong College for higher secondary study. I admitted to BUET in the year 2015. My hobby is playing video games and travelling. My research field is about the mechanical and thermal stress migration of the metal matrix composites.
